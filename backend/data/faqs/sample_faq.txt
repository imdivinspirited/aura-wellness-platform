FAQ – Art of Living

Q: What is Sudarshan Kriya?
A: Sudarshan Kriya is a unique breathing technique taught in the Art of Living courses. It involves specific rhythms of breath that help release stress and bring the mind to the present moment.

Q: Where is the Bangalore Ashram?
A: The Art of Living International Center (Bangalore Ashram) is located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. Address and directions are available on the official website.

Q: How can I register for a course?
A: You can register online at www.artofliving.org or contact your local Art of Living center. Phone: +91-80-67262626, Email: info@artofliving.org.

Q: Are courses available in Hindi?
A: Yes, courses are conducted in multiple languages including English, Hindi, and regional languages depending on the location and batch.
