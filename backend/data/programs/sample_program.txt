Sri Sri Yoga

Sri Sri Yoga is a program that combines yoga asanas, pranayama, and meditation. It is designed for all levels and helps improve flexibility, strength, and mental clarity.

Duration: Usually 2-3 days or spread over several weeks in weekly sessions.
Benefits: Stress relief, better sleep, improved focus, physical wellness.
Founder: Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar.

For schedules and registration visit https://www.artofliving.org or contact your nearest Art of Living center.
