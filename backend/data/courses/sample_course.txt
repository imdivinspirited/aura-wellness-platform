Art of Living Happiness Program

The Happiness Program is a comprehensive course that introduces participants to Sudarshan Kriya, a powerful breathing technique. The program runs over 3 days (approx 6 hours per day) and includes:

- Yoga and meditation sessions
- Knowledge sessions on stress and mind
- Sudarshan Kriya practice
- Group processes and discussions

Schedule: Typically offered on weekends. Contact your local center for dates.
Fees: Vary by location. Please check the website or contact info@artofliving.org.
Location: Available at Art of Living centers worldwide including Bangalore Ashram.
