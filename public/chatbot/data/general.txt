Art of Living International Center
The Art of Living Foundation was founded by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar. It offers programs for stress relief, meditation, yoga, and breathing techniques including the SKY (Sudarshan Kriya) practice.

Happiness Program
The Happiness Program is a comprehensive course that introduces participants to meditation, breathing techniques, and practical wisdom for daily life. It includes SKY (Sudarshan Kriya), which has been researched for benefits in reducing stress and improving well-being.

Sri Sri Yoga
Sri Sri Yoga combines asanas (postures), pranayama (breathing), and meditation. It is suitable for all levels and promotes physical and mental well-being.

Sahaj Samadhi Meditation
An effortless meditation technique that brings deep rest and inner silence. It is designed to reduce stress and enhance clarity and focus.

Bangalore Ashram
The Art of Living International Center is located on the outskirts of Bangalore, India. It offers residential programs, retreats, and daily activities including meditation, satsang, and seva (voluntary service).

Programs and Courses
Art of Living offers programs for beginners (Happiness Program, Sri Sri Yoga), advanced courses (Advanced Meditation Program, DSN), corporate wellness, and programs for children and youth.

Contact and Visit
To plan a visit or get information about programs, you can contact the ashram via the Connect page. The ashram is open to visitors and offers accommodation and food for program participants.

Seva and Careers
Seva means selfless service. The ashram offers opportunities for seva, paid jobs, and internships. Applications can be submitted through the Seva & Careers page on the website.
